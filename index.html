What is a Guarantor Loan?

A guarantor loan is an unsecured loan where the borrower nominates someone else to make payments if they are unable to. As this lowers the risk for default for lenders, it may increase your chances of approval if you have bad credit.

Buddy Loans are a guarantor loan company where you can borrow from £1,000 up to £10,000, and pay it back over a period you can afford between 12 and 60 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Guarantor Loan?

A guarantor loan is an unsecured personal loan between £1000 and £10,000 taken over a period of 12-60 months. The only difference is that, due to the borrower's credit risk we ask for another person to guarantee that the loan will be repaid.

What is a Guarantor Loan for?

  • - Someone who may not have any credit and is looking to build a credit history
  • - Anyone who may be in need of money quickly, without loads of paperwork
  • - Someone who may have had bad credit in the past

Does a Guarantor loan require a credit score check?

We will consider your credit history and the credit history of your guarantor, however your application approval will be primarily based on your ability to repay your loan.

How long does it take to complete my application?

The application process is easy and straightforward. In most instances applications are approved within 24 hours, although it can be as quick the same day.

Can I settle my loan early?

Yes! If you wish to pay your loan off early you can do so with no early penalties.

What happens if I cannot afford to repay my Loan?

If you are, or believe you may start, encountering any issues with being able to meet your repayments we would urge you to contact us as soon as possible. If you miss a payment, we will then contact the guarantor and request payment from them.

Do you charge me for taking out a Buddy Loan?

We don't apply any fees or charges for your application or taking out a Buddy Loan. Your interest will accrue on a daily basis in line with that outlined in your credit agreement and each payment you make will be made up of paying your interest and a part towards the capital.

I need to sort my loan quickly, where can I go?

Yes, Guarantor Loans are an unsecured loan and do not require any collateral or property to be secured against. You just need a good buddy who agrees to support your loan.

I just need a small loan, say £1,000 to £1,500

At Buddy Loans you can borrow as little as £1000 up to £10,000. This doesn't mean you have to borrow £10,000, you can borrow as little as £1,000, if that is all you require.

How do I pay back my Guarantor Loan?

Payments are on a monthly basis. Your payments will be determined by the amount you wish to borrow, and the term of the loan. The term is how long you are borrowing the money for. The longer the term of your loan such as 48 months of payments instead of 36 months of payments will reduce your monthly payment. However, you will pay more in interest over the longer time period.
